    Description

    As the Director of Enterprise Insights and Analytics, you will lead the organization toward new opportunities for revenue growth, brand strengthening and market positioning.  You will deliver measurable insights in the enterprise data set and in vendor data sets for end users to address immediate business issues.  You will take a collaborative approach in managing projects for all corporate departments and the field to develop and support strategies that deliver high value to the team and NOI growth to the company. 

    We will look to you to coordinate with functional business units on analytics and insights, dashboard training and effective utilization.  You will also evaluate the consumer market and retail landscapes and make recommendations to increase efficiency in Tanger centers. If you are an analytical individual who thrives on solving business challenges, a forward thinker, and someone who enjoys working in a fast-pace environment, then this is the job for you. 

    We offer excellent benefits for our full-time team members, to list a few:

    • Competitive salary
    • Medical, Dental, and Vision coverage
    • 401(k) with generous match
    • Paid PTO, Holidays and Bereavement Leave
    • Paid volunteer hours
    • Tuition Reimbursement
    • Wellness Incentives

    How can you contribute to what we do?

    • Your strong problem-solving abilities will help you develop and maintain deep subject matter expertise of relevant consumer/business segments, market dynamics and trends.
    • With your strong analytical abilities, you will contribute to and direct broad scale research initiatives, design sessions and ad hoc projects across all methodologies. (quantitative, qualitative, ethnography, panel, focus groups, etc.)
    • Your proficiency with large data analytics will help you develop custom analytics using algorithms and advanced statistical models.
    • You will enhance data visualization through the development of custom dashboards and maps.
    • With your strong analytical skills, you will analyze and validate raw survey data.
    • Through collaboration, you will work with vendor teams for research and insights, work with programmers to QC and test existing products and identify and prioritize new features for development.
    • You will conduct robust research and analytics projects across key areas of the business. Organize and coordinate projects so budgets and results are met.
    • Through your research, you will translate and simplify market insights for reporting and communicating signaling metrics – internal and external.
    • You will provide forecast analysis for all retail partners as needed, track retail sales for all brands in Tanger centers and innovate new tools for monitoring sales.
    • You will build and cultivate relationships with all functional groups and understand key use cases for data, anticipate needs and continually reinforce our position as the “go-to” group for consumer and market analytics. Participate in meetings within the functional groups as needed.
    • You will develop high quality, end user-ready analysis for senior review that effectively communicates critical learnings, insights and conclusions which drive impactful recommendations.
    • You will contribute to the preparation and production of persona data for existing personas and develop new personas as data becomes available.
    • By preparing data-based insights and recommendations you will assist in the successful delivery of optimal consumer segments for Tanger.
    • You will maintain a solid understanding of enterprise goals and prepare datasets that assist in extracting the most value from retailer clients and centers.

    What skills are necessary for success?

    • You have strong analytical skills and have an innate ability to synthesize complex information.
    • You have an impeccable ability to problem solve in a fluid, high output work environment.
    • You have the ability to develop strong relationships and communicate and educate on  data effectively to end users.
    • You have a thirst for knowledge and actively adopt new technologies.
    • You maintain a proficiency in analyzing large data analytics and location-based datasets.
    • You utilize ESRI suite products including ArcGIS.
    • Your education includes a bachelor’s degree from an accredited university in Marketing, Applied Statistics, Mathematics, Management Science, Engineering, Economics, Social Sciences or other relevant business field.
    • You have 5+ years of experience in analytics consulting, including supplier side, account management and/or consulting service.
    • You have 3+ years of self-guided analysis with working knowledge of business analytics with proficiency in ESRI, MRI, TruTrade and traditional research methodologies for the shopping center industry.

    Who will you work with?

    • You will collaborate with all functional business units, field teams, retailers and research agencies.

    Partner with Tanger Outlets and be a part of our extraordinary team!

    Tanger Factory Outlet Centers, Inc. (NYSE: SKT) is a leading operator of open-air upscale outlet shopping centers that owns, or has an ownership interest in, a portfolio of 37 centers. Tanger’s operating properties are located in 20 states and in Canada, totaling approximately 14.1 million square feet, leased to over 2,600 stores operated by more than 500 different brand name companies. The Company has more than 40 years of experience in the outlet industry and is a publicly-traded REIT.
